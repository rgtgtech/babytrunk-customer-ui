A premium made in India luxury gifting brand for new borns

Baby Trunk offers modern yet classic, world class products. The brand commits to be socially and ethically responsible to create safe,green and chemical free products for globe trotting mothers, who choose to redefine luxury by making responsible choices, in order to create a better and healthier tomorrow for their young ones.

Our Vision

To fulfill the aspirational needs of the childhood clothing by offering international stardards in quality, craftmanship & service and be the distinctive mark of grandeour in luxury lifestyle space of home and accessories, symbolized by the crest.

Our Mission

Maintain the position of being the most profitable lifestyle brand inthe super premium baby wear category. Launch of The Baby trunk of kidswear (age 1 year +) over the next two years through renewed focus.

The Support Team

It was not an easy decision to create yet another lifestyle brand. We asked overselves. Do we really need more product? but ultimately The Baby Trunk is a vehicle to communicate a philosophy of living. We felt the fast fashion pendulum was swinging too far and yearned for products that are designed to last, not trend-driven and inspired by natural design and materials.We wanted to design the products we'd use ourselves but just could not find here in India. ones that reflected our history and yet were global their appeal.
